QUESTIONS FROM THE GRAVE

WHAT IS DEAD WEST COIN?

Dead West Coin is a collection of 10,000 unique Western-theme zombie NFTs on the Solana blockchain. Each NFT is algorithmically generated from hundreds of hand-drawn assets and gives you access to our growing Dead West universe.

HOW MUCH DOES IT COST TO MINT?

The mint price is 0.69 SOL (nice) and the reveal will happen 24 hours after mint-out. There are no bonding curves or price increases - the mint price stays the same until the last NFT is claimed.

WHEN IS THE MINT DATE?

The zombies rise on July 18th, 2023 at 12:00 PM EST. Set a reminder and make sure your SOL is ready!

HOW DO I PREPARE FOR MINT?

1. Get a Solana wallet like Phantom or Solflare
2. Buy SOL and have at least 0.7 SOL in your wallet (for mint + small tx fee)
3. Connect your wallet when the countdown hits 0
4. Click MINT and approve the transaction
5. Watch as your zombie cowboy joins the horde!
